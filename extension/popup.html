Veritas

Paper quality scoring & citation verification

How to use

Open your paper on Overleaf or Google Docs. Click "Analyze Document" in the Veritas dashboard (bottom-right corner) to score your sections.

CLI also available

npm install -g veritas-cli
veritas analyze paper.md
veritas audit-citations paper.md
veritas repair paper.md --fix-citations
